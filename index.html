Our Investment Philosophy
Before we invest a single dollar,
we build the right foundation.
At Capital Planning Wealth Management, every financial plan begins with one question: when will you need this money? That answer determines everything — which bucket it belongs in, how much risk is appropriate, and how we respond when markets get uncomfortable.
The Capital Planning Three-Bucket Framework

Safety & Liquidity

Your financial safety net — money that must be there no matter what. This bucket carries zero market risk. Stability and immediate access are the only goals.
